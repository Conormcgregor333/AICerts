Event List

The AI Innovators Summit 2024 brings together the brightest minds in artificial intelligence to explore the latest advancements and their impact on various industries. This event will feature keynote presentations from leading AI researchers and industry pioneers, interactive workshops, and panel discussions that delve into AI's role in healthcare, finance, education, and more. Attendees will have the opportunity to network with AI professionals, participate in hands-on sessions, and discover cutting-edge technologies that are shaping the future.

Join us at the Global AI and Machine Learning Conference 2024, where experts from around the world converge to share knowledge, discuss trends, and explore the future of AI and machine learning. This conference will cover a wide range of topics, including deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and AI ethics. Participants will benefit from in-depth technical sessions, case studies, and hands-on workshops designed to enhance their understanding and application of AI technologies.

The AI for Social Good Symposium 2024 is dedicated to exploring how artificial intelligence can be leveraged to address some of the world's most pressing social challenges. This event will highlight projects and initiatives that use AI to improve public health, promote education, support environmental sustainability, and advance social justice. Attendees will hear from thought leaders, policymakers, and practitioners who are pioneering AI applications for the greater good. The symposium will feature inspiring talks, collaborative workshops, and networking opportunities designed to foster partnerships and inspire action.
